# Doctor Outreach Message Templates

Use these templates to reach out to potential pilot doctors for Lipi.

---

## Option 1: Warm Intro (Personal Connection / Colleague)

> "Hi Dr. [Name], hope you are doing well! 
>
> I've been working on a clinical assistant called **Lipi** that helps doctors reduce their daily documentation overhead. It listens to clinical conversations in Hindi/Hinglish/English and automatically drafts complete SOAP notes and prescriptions in seconds.
>
> We are running a 14-day observation pilot to test it in real OPD settings and would love to get your feedback. We handle all patient privacy requirements, delete the audio immediately after note generation, and scrub personal details. 
>
> Let me know if we can do a quick 5-minute call or if I can set up a demo for you. Thanks!"

## Option 3: Formal Email

> **Subject: Reducing OPD Documentation Overhead by 70% with Lipi (AI Scribe for Indian Clinics)**
>
> Dear Dr. [Name],
>
> I hope this email finds you well.
>
> Many physicians in Indian OPDs spend 3-4 hours daily manually writing or typing patient notes. To address this, we developed **Lipi**, a multilingual clinical voice assistant that automatically converts spoken doctor-patient consultations (in Hindi, Hinglish, or English) into structured SOAP notes, prescriptions, and FHIR-compliant records.
>
> We are launching a **14-Day Pilot Observation Sprint** to validate Lipi's accuracy and usability in active clinics. 
>
> **Lipi Key Highlights:**
> 1. **Multilingual ASR**: High accuracy on Hinglish, local Hindi dialects, and English terminology.
> 2. **Strict Privacy**: Audio is processed in India and deleted immediately after note generation. Protected Health Information (PHI) is scrubbed locally before any storage.
> 3. **Assistive Decision Support**: Flags potential allergy conflicts and drug-drug interactions for review.
>
> We would be honored to invite you to participate in this pilot. The setup takes less than 10 minutes, and we will handle all observer coordination.
>
> Would you have 10 minutes next Tuesday or Wednesday for a brief virtual demonstration?
>
> Sincerely,  
> [Your Name]  
> Lipi Project Lead
