# Doctor Consent Script & Agreement (Pilot Observation Sprint)

Lipi is a multilingual clinical decision support system research prototype. By participating in this observation sprint, the participating physician agrees to the following conditions:

---

## Agreement & Responsibilities

1. **Lipi is Assistive Only**: Lipi is not a certified medical device and does not make autonomous clinical diagnoses or treatment decisions.
2. **Mandatory Review**: The physician is the final authority on everything. You must review, correct, and sign off on every generated SOAP note, medication list, and clinical recommendation before applying them to a patient's chart.
3. **Verbal Consent Enforcement**: The physician agrees to ask for verbal patient consent before every recorded session and verify it in the Lipi UI.
4. **Data Security**: Audio recordings are processed on secure Indian servers (Sarvam AI) for ASR and deleted immediately after the notes are generated. Transcripts are scrubbed of PHI (Protected Health Information) locally before storage.
5. **No Hallucination Liability**: The research team is not liable for clinical errors or hallucinations in the draft SOAP note or CDS suggestions.
