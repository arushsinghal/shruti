# Patient Consent Script

This script is to be spoken to the patient before initiating any session recording or manual transcript submission in Lipi.

---

## Hindi Script (Recommended for Hindi/Hinglish OPDs)

> **"Namaste. Main humari aaj ki baat-cheet ko briefly record karunga taaki aapki medical file (SOAP note) bilkul sahi aur tezi se ban sake. Aapki yeh recording safe rahegi aur isse koi bhi personal details (jaise naam ya phone) store karne se pehle mita diye jayenge. Kya main record kar sakta hoon?"**

---

## English Script

> **"Hello. I will briefly record our conversation to help me write accurate clinical notes for your medical record. Your voice and data are protected, and any personal details (like names, numbers, or dates) will be scrubbed before the notes are stored. Do I have your permission to record?"**

---

## Important Rules

1. **Explicit Verbal Consent**: Audio recording must NOT start until the patient has given clear verbal consent.
2. **Right to Withdraw**: Patients must be informed that they can ask to stop the recording or delete the session data at any time.
3. **No Coercion**: Consent must be voluntary. A patient's refusal to consent must not affect their quality of care.
4. **Consent Gate**: Confirm the consent in the Lipi app interface to unlock the audio controls.
